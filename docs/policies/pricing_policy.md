# Pricing Policy & Guidelines

## Core Pricing Rules

1.  **Minimum Margin**: No product shall be sold below a **10% gross margin**.
    -   *Formula*: `(Price - Cost) / Price >= 0.10`
    -   *Exception*: Clearance items (requires Manager Approval).

2.  **Competitor Matching**:
    -   We aim to match valid competitor prices within **5%**.
    -   Do **NOT** match competitors if it violates the Minimum Margin rule.

3.  **Max Price Increase**:
    -   Price increases are capped at **20%** per day to prevent customer shock.

4.  **Global Price Cap**:
    -   No item should ever exceed **$1,000.00** base price without Executive Review.

## Special Logic

-   **Stock Low (< 10 units)**: A **5% surge premium** is allowed and encouraged.
-   **High Demand (Views > 100/5min)**: Small incremental increases (1-2%) are permitted every hour.
